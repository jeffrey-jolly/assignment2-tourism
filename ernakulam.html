Ernakulam

With one leg in the past, and other in the future, Ernakulam is a heady mix of modern, and ancient tourist attractions. There's nowhere else in India one can find gigantic business hubs sharing space with a 400-year-old synagogue, ancient mosques, Portuguese houses and the crumbling sites of Business Raj. Moreover, what attracts tourists the most to Ernakulam are the Keralan art shows.There is so much to see and explore in Ernakulam, it has various historical sites, temples, forts, beaches, museums, and other tourist places to attract travelers from all around the world. No doubt that Ernakulam is an ultimate destination for a globetrotter, who aims to explore as much as they can in their single visit to 'God's Own Country.
